02. Project Vision & Objectives

Understanding the problem we're solving and the goals that guide development decisions.

The Problem

Women's health conditions like breast cancer, PCOS, and uterine fibroids affect millions worldwide. Early detection is critical for successful treatment, but several challenges persist:

  • Lack of Expert Access: Specialized radiologists and gynecologists are scarce in rural areas of Andhra Pradesh
  • Delayed Diagnosis: Manual video analysis takes significant time, delaying treatment
  • Inconsistent Results: Human fatigue and subjectivity can affect diagnostic accuracy
  • Data Silos: Lack of centralized health data prevents effective resource allocation
  • Limited Tracking: No comprehensive system for monitoring health trends across districts

Our Solution

SETV Women Wellness addresses these challenges by providing:

🤖 AI-Assisted Diagnosis
Upload ultrasound video and get AI analysis in real-time—no waiting for manual review
📋 Comprehensive Reports
Detailed PDF reports with findings, doctor's comments, and treatment recommendations
🗺️ District Monitoring
State-wide health dashboard with interactive heatmap and analytics
📱 Works Anywhere
Responsive web app works on any device with a modern browser

Project Objectives

# Objective Success Metric
1 Achieve high accuracy detection ≥90% accuracy on test videos
2 Provide real-time analysis <5 second per frame processing
3 Support multiple conditions Breast cancer, PCOS, Fibroids
4 Mobile-first accessibility 100% responsive on all devices
5 Comprehensive reporting PDF with frames, analysis, comments
6 District-wide monitoring All 13 AP districts covered
7 Easy deployment Single-command start

Design Principles

  1. Healthcare Professional-Centric: Every feature considers the end-user—medical staff with varying tech experience

const bodyParser = require('body-parser');

require('./models/db');

const PORT = process.env.PORT || 8080;

// CORS configuration
app.use(cors());

// Body parsing with increased limits for video uploads
app.use(express.json({ limit: '50mb' }));
app.use(express.urlencoded({

PART VI: DISEASE DATABASE

31. Breast Cancer Overview

Detailed information on breast cancer detection capabilities in SETV Women Wellness.

🔴 Malignant Breast Tumor
SEVERITY: CRITICAL

Malignant breast tumors are cancerous growths that can invade nearby tissue and spread to other parts of the body. Early detection is critical for successful treatment outcomes.

Ultrasound Characteristics:

  • Irregular or spiculated margins
  • Heterogeneous internal echoes
  • Posterior acoustic shadowing
  • Taller than wide orientation
  • Angular margins
  • Microlobulations

Risk Factors:

  • Age over 50
  • Family history of breast cancer
  • BRCA1/BRCA2 gene mutations
  • Dense breast tissue
  • Previous breast biopsies
  • Hormone replacement therapy

Recommended Actions:

  • Immediate consultation with oncologist
  • Biopsy for histological confirmation
  • Additional imaging (mammography, MRI)
  • Staging workup if confirmed malignant
🟢 Benign Breast Tumor
SEVERITY: LOW

Benign breast tumors are non-cancerous growths that do not spread to other parts of the body. While typically not life-threatening, they may require monitoring or treatment depending on size and symptoms.

Ultrasound Characteristics:

  • Smooth, well-circumscribed margins
  • Homogeneous internal echoes
  • Posterior acoustic enhancement
  • Wider than tall orientation
  • Thin echogenic capsule

Common Types:

  • Fibroadenoma: Most common in women under 30
  • Cysts: Fluid-filled sacs
  • Papillomas: Wart-like growths in ducts
  • Fat necrosis: From injury to fatty tissue

Recommended Actions:

  • Regular monitoring (6-12 months)
  • Ultrasound-guided aspiration for cysts
  • Surgical removal if symptomatic
  • Annual mammography for ongoing surveillance
